## 1. PreLegal — AI Powered Legal Document Drafting

- **Description**: A full-stack SaaS platform that uses conversational AI to draft professional legal agreements in minutes. Supports 11 document types — including NDAs, Data Processing Agreements, and HIPAA Business Associate Agreements — based on Common Paper open standards vetted by 40+ attorneys.
- **Problem**: Most standard business agreements don't need a lawyer to draft from scratch, but the process is slow, expensive, and inaccessible for early-stage companies and founders.
- **Solution**: Built a conversational drafting experience where users describe the agreement in plain English, the AI asks the right questions, and a clean legal document builds in real time. Users can save drafts, edit them, and export to PDF. Includes real user authentication, document persistence, and a fully automated CI/CD pipeline deploying to Render.
- **Technologies**: Python, FastAPI, Next.js, Docker, Claude (via OpenRouter), GitHub Actions, Render

[GitHub](https://github.com/anirudhsshirahati/prelegal) | [Live App](https://prelegal-t4hf.onrender.com)

## 2. Loss Ratio Prediction for Auto Insurance

- **Description**: A machine learning project using an auto insurance dataset to predict loss ratios for insurance policy portfolios.
- **Problem**: Insurance pricing and risk assessment depends on accurately forecasting how much a portfolio will pay out relative to premiums collected. Poor predictions lead to underpriced risk and financial exposure.
- **Solution**: Applied the CRISP-DM methodology through the full project lifecycle — from business understanding to model evaluation. Performed data preparation, exploratory analysis, feature engineering, and regression modeling to build a reliable prediction pipeline.
- **Technologies**: Python, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Docker, GCP

## 4. Bank Marketing Analysis

- **Solution**: Performed data preprocessing and exploratory analysis, computed fairness metrics, and identified areas of potential bias in the dataset and model predictions, providing actionable insight for model improvement.
